About AI Interview App

The AI Interview App is designed to streamline the interview process by leveraging artificial intelligence. Our app allows you to conduct online interviews, record both video and screen of the user, and provide insightful analytics to help you make informed decisions.

Our mission is to make the interview process more efficient and effective for both interviewers and candidates. By using AI, we can provide a more consistent and unbiased evaluation of candidates, ensuring that you find the best fit for your team.
